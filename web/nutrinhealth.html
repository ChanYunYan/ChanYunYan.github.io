Nutritions & Health of Dogs

Balanced Diet:

Dogs require a mix of proteins, carbohydrates, fats,

vitamins, and minerals.

FATS:

Important for energy and skin health. Omega fatty

acids are particularly beneficial.

Carbohydrates:

Provide energy and should come from whole grains

and vegetables.

Proteins:

For growth, maintenance, and repair of tissues.

Sources include meat, fish, and eggs.
